PT Unilever Indonesia Tbk

PT Unilever Indonesia Tbk. is a subsidiary of Unilever, a leading global consumer company focused on a wide range of household, food, beverage and personal care products. Unilever has been operating in Indonesia since 1933. PT Unilever Indonesia Tbk. was founded in 1933 and has grown to become one of the leaders in the consumer industry in Indonesia. Unilever Indonesia produces various kinds of products that are widely known by the Indonesian people. This includes personal care products such as soap, shampoo, toothpaste (branded products such as Lifebuoy, Sunsilk, and Pepsodent), food and beverage products such as tea (branded products such as SariWangi), frozen foods (branded products such as Wall's), and various products household (brand products such as Rinso and Cif).
