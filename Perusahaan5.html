PT Mahakarya Sukses Indonesia

Having been involved in the IT business since the late 1980s, the ITSC management team has also grown and transformed the company into one of the leading IT service providers in Indonesia. Apart from being one of the most established Authorized Service Centers for many of the world's leading IT vendors, ITSC also has the capability to provide professional services in the areas of technology related to Telecommunications, Fleet Management and IoT-based devices.
With offices in more than 34 provinces and a team of trained engineers, ITSC has the resources and capabilities to support our customers' needs and requirements in most parts of Indonesia, providing them with the highest level of customer service. Our ability to support a wide geographic coverage in Indonesia is further enhanced through our partnerships with more than 300 local IT companies.
