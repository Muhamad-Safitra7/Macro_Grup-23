PT Pertamina (Persero)

Pertamina, officially known as PT Pertamina (Persero) is a leading oil and gas company in Indonesia which is also a State-Owned Enterprise (BUMN). Pertamina was founded on December 10 1957 and is the successor to the General Oil and Gas Leadership Agency (BPUMGB) which previously managed the oil and gas industry in Indonesia. Companies have an important role in managing the country's oil and gas resources. Pertamina is a major player in the Indonesian oil and gas industry. They are involved in exploration, production, processing, distribution and sales of petroleum, natural gas and their derivative products. Pertamina is engaged in the exploration and production of petroleum and natural gas both domestically and abroad. They partner with international energy companies to develop exploration projects.
