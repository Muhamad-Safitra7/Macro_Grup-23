PT Garuda Indonesia (Persero) Tbk

Garuda Indonesia, or PT Garuda Indonesia (Persero) Tbk., is the national airline of Indonesia and one of the largest airlines in Southeast Asia. Garuda Indonesia was founded in 1947 as Indonesia's national airline. Since then, the company has experienced various stages of development and expansion. Garuda Indonesia is a member of the star airline alliance "SkyTeam", which includes several of the world's leading airlines. This membership allows Garuda to offer better global connectivity to its passengers. Garuda Indonesia has a modern fleet consisting of sophisticated aircraft including Boeing 777, Airbus A330, Boeing 737, and others. This fleet allows Garuda to provide comfortable and safe flight services.
