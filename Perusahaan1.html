PT Mahakarya Sukses Indonesia

PT MAHAKARYA SUKSES INDONESIA (MITO Group), a company engaged in Telecommunications and Electronics. One of its product brands is MITO. MITO is one of the pioneers of the first national local brand cellphone in Indonesia since 2006. Entering the end of 2012, MITO managed to become the Top 3 largest mobile phone vendor in Indonesia with higher market share growth and even distribution channels.
A series of achievements have been achieved by MITO (The Most Favorite, Best Seller Mobile, The Most Innovative Phone) and become partners of some of the largest telecommunications operators in Indonesia. Until now MITO is one of the largest local cellphone manufacturers with after-sales services spread throughout Indonesia.
