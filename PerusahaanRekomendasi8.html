PT Indofood Sukses Makmur Tbk

PT Indofood Sukses Makmur Tbk. is one of the largest food companies in Indonesia and one of the major players in the food and agribusiness industry in Southeast Asia. Indofood was founded in 1990 and since then has grown to become the largest food company in Indonesia. The company is part of the Salim group, which also has interests in various industries. Indofood has a very broad product portfolio, covering various food and beverage categories. Its products include instant noodles (brand products like Indomie), canned foods, cereals, cooking oil, processed meat products, dairy products, and more. Indofood has several leading brands in Indonesia, such as Indomie (a very popular instant noodle), Teh Sosro, Pop Mie, and many more. These brands have become households in Indonesia and gained recognition at the international level.
