PT Fintek Karya Nusantara

PT Fintek Karya Nusantara, is one of the leading digital payment service providers in Indonesia. LinkAja was established in 2019 as a result of cooperation between several large companies in Indonesia, including SOEs (State-Owned Enterprises) and private companies. The goal is to develop comprehensive digital payment services and facilitate access to financial services for Indonesians. LinkAja is a digital payment platform that provides a variety of financial services, including bill payments, money transfers, credit purchases, public transportation payments, grocery payments, and more. They also integrate various financial services on one platform, making it easier for users to perform various financial transactions. LinkAja can be accessed through an easy-to-use mobile application. The app allows users to make financial transactions, check balances, and access various other services quickly and securely.
