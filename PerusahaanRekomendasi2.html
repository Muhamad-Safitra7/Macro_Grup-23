PT Indosat Tbk

Indosat Ooredoo, or PT Indosat Tbk., is one of the leading telecommunications operators in Indonesia. The company is part of the Ooredoo group, based in Qatar, and provides a variety of telecommunications services, including mobile, landline and data services. Indosat Ooredoo was founded in 1967 as the first telecommunications operator in Indonesia. Since then, this company has grown rapidly and become one of the key players in the telecommunications industry in Indonesia. Indosat Ooredoo offers a variety of cellular services, including prepaid and postpaid services, for individual and business customers. They also provide 4G and 5G services to improve data connectivity. The company has an extensive network infrastructure, covering all regions of Indonesia. This includes thousands of cell towers and other telecommunications facilities.
