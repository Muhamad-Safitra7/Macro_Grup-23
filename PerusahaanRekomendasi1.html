PT Aplikasi Karya Anak Bangsa

PT Application Karya Anak Bangsa or better known as Gojek is one of the leading technology companies in Indonesia. Founded in 2010 by Nadiem Makarim, Kevin Aluwi, and Michaelangelo Moran, Gojek first emerged as a ride-sharing service, allowing users to order motorbikes or cars for their trips. However, over time, Gojek developed into an integrated service platform that provides a variety of services, including food delivery, online shopping, goods delivery and financial services.
