PT Asuransi FPG Indonesia

Founded in 1866, PT Asuransi FPG Indonesia, previously known as PT Asuransi Indrapura Indonesia, has built an enviable reputation for developing deep customer insights that support the creation of relevant and comprehensive commercial and individual insurance products and solutions. PT Asuransi FPG Indonesia maintains strong alliances with global institutions, which when combined with our local expertise, will provide intelligent structured solutions for the Indonesian insurance market. With a network of FPG Insurance Branches in 17 major cities and supported by the Zuellig Group of Companies, our team of experienced and talented professionals are experts in the implementation of bespoke insurance and risk management solutions for our customers.
PT Asuransi FPG Indonesia is a trusted name in providing general insurance solutions in Indonesia and the Asia Pacific region.
